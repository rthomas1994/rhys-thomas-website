About Me

Rhys Thomas Web Designer & Front End Developer rhysthomas1994@gmail.com

Hello. I am a web designer & front end developer based in Long Island, NY. I grew up in England and graduated with a degree in Business Management from the University of Sheffield in 2017, before moving to the US in 2018.

My passion for UI/UX stems from my time managing projects in a web implementation team. I quickly realized that I had minimal interest in running the projects, but instead found myself fascinated by the project work itself. I began researching, learning, and developing my passion, before moving to a full-time position in front end design and development shortly after.
